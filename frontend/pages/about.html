Our Mission

At SEALit, we aim to revolutionize product authenticity and trust by leveraging blockchain technology. Our platform ensures that every product registered with us is verified and protected against counterfeiting.

How It Works

Sellers can register their products on our platform, and each product is assigned a unique QR code. Buyers can scan this QR code to verify the authenticity of the product and access its full history.

Why Choose Us?

Transparency : Every product's history is stored on the blockchain, ensuring complete transparency. Security : Our system is tamper-proof, making it impossible for counterfeiters to manipulate data. Trust : Build trust with your customers by providing verified and authentic products.
