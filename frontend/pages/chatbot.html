What if there's no QR code? The bag only has a serial number.

12:04 PM

If the product only has a serial number, you can still verify it:

  1. Click on "Verify Product" in the main menu
  2. Select the "Manual Entry" option
  3. Enter the serial number and product details
  4. Our system will check against registered authentic products

Not all products have DNA tags or QR codes yet, especially older items. If verification fails, it doesn't automatically mean the product is fake - it might just not be registered in our system.
